Welcome to the website of GEBA

The Georgian Biotechnology Association unites professionals, researchers, and students who are dedicated to promoting the development of biotechnology in Georgia.

About Us

Our mission is to advance biotechnology, strengthen international collaboration, and engage young scientists in research and innovation.

Our Activities

  • Promoting biotechnology research and innovation
  • Facilitating collaboration between academia, industry, and government institutions
  • Organising educational and public awareness programmes
  • Supporting young scientists and students
