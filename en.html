About us

The Association aims to support the development of biotechnology in Georgia, promote cooperation between academia, industry and governmental sectors, and encourage young scientists to engage in scientific activities.

Our Activities

  • Supporting biotechnological research and innovation
  • Facilitating collaboration between academia, industry and government
  • Raising public awareness about biotechnology
  • Encouraging student/scientist development through education
