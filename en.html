About GEBA

The Georgian Biotechnology Association (GEBA) is a non-profit organisation committed to supporting the development and promotion of biotechnology in Georgia. We bring together researchers, professionals, and organisations working in biotechnology and related fields to strengthen innovation, education, and sustainable development.

Our Activities

  • Promoting biotechnology research and innovation
  • Facilitating collaboration between academia, industry, and government institutions
  • Organising educational and public awareness programmes
  • Supporting young scientists and students
